Patient and Records

Doctors and hospitals need to keep accurate records of all their patients. This is
essential to ensure correct diagnosis and treatment. Databases are kept by doctora
and hospitals so that data can be shared between medical practitioners and pharmacies
The data required on a patient data base would be:
  1. A unique identification number
  2. Name and adress
  3. Data of birth
  4. Gender
  5. Medical history
  6. Blood group
  7. Any known allergies
  8. Doctor
  9. Any current treatment
  10. Any current diagnosis
  11. Important additional information (X-rays or CT scans
