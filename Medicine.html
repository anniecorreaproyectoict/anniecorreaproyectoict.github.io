Patient and Records

Doctors and hospitals need to keep accurate records of all their patients. This is
essential to ensure correct diagnosis and treatment. Databases are kept by doctora
and hospitals so that data can be shared between medical practitioners and pharmacies
The data required on a patient data base would be:
  1. A unique identification number
  2. Name and adress
  3. Data of birth
  4. Gender
  5. Medical history
  6. Blood group
  7. Any known allergies
  8. Doctor
  9. Any current treatment
  10. Any current diagnosis
  11. Important additional information (X-rays or CT scans

Prosthetics

3D printers are now being used to print out prosthetics such as false arms, hands and legs.
While state-of-the-art myoelectric prosthetics cost tens of thousands of dollars, the price
for 3D-printing a prosthetic arm or handcan be as little as $100.

Tissue engineering

Recent advances have allowed the 3D printing of bio-compatible materials, cells and supporting
structures. This has enabled the viability of producing artificial cells and tissues within 3D
printed object.
